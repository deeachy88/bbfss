General Entry Conditions :

The consignment of food should –

1. Be accompanied by Bhutan Food Safety Certificate (Annexure 1) only for high risk products.

2. Meet National Food Labelling Requirements

3. Have atleast 50% of its shelf life during the time of import

4. The consignment of Food Lot must be unloaded from transport for inspection and or sampling (if required)

5. Evidence they are in good regulatory standing from responsible authority in exporting country

6. For first time imports from new suppliers, please attach a copy of the last inspection report

7. Transported in appropriate transport condition

The importer must:

• advise BAFRA of any circumstance that may affect the safety and suitability of the food.

The import permit may be revoked where BAFRA determines that:

• the zoonotic disease outbreak has occurred in the exporting country

• there is a food safety problem in the exporting country or the processor is no longer in good regulatory standing,

• Importer is determined to be non-compliant following an inspection
